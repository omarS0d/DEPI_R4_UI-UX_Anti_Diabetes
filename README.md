##  Project Overview
A UI/UX project that aims to help all diabetes patients across the world to reclaik their lives and set free from the chains holding them back from enjoying food by organizing and recommending their meals and keeping track of their hydration level,insulin level and activity,by analysing the UX of diabetes patients ,collecting data and creating screens of the final app

---

## Project Objectives
- Improve self-management of diabetes patients 
- Provide personalized food and meal recommendations  
- Help users monitor insulin and hydration levels 
- Increase awareness of healthy lifestyle habits  
---

##  Project Scope
- designing a user-friendly UI/UX system to help diabetes patients monitor their insulin and blood sugar levels
- organizes food and provides simple meal recommendations. 
- supports hydration tracking and healthy daily habits.
- focuses on easy, clear, and accessible user experience

>  **Final Deliverable:**
> - A complete high-fidelity UI/UX design for a diabetes management application
> - A UX research report including user journey, analysis of diabetes patient posts, and usability insights.
